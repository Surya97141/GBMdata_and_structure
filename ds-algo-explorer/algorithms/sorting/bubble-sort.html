Bubble Sort

The classic "let the biggest bubble to the top" algorithm.

Bubble Sort repeatedly steps through the array comparing adjacent elements and swapping them when out of order. After each full pass the largest unsorted element has "bubbled" to its final position at the end.

An optimised version terminates early when a pass completes with zero swaps — giving O(n) on already-sorted data. That optimisation is included in this simulation.

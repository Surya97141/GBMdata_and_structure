Pascal's Triangle

C(n,k) = C(n-1,k-1) + C(n-1,k) — builds binomial coefficients row by row.

Pascal's triangle computes all binomial coefficients C(n,k) using the recurrence C(n,k) = C(n-1,k-1) + C(n-1,k). Row n contains coefficients of (a+b)^n. Edges are always 1. Interior cells are the sum of the two cells directly above.

Yellow = cell being computed. Purple = the two parent cells it depends on. Green = already done.

Time
O(n²)
Space
O(n²)
Row n sum
2^n

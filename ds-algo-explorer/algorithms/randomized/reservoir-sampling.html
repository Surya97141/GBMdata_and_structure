Reservoir Sampling

Sample k items uniformly from a stream of unknown size — O(n) time, O(k) space.

Reservoir sampling (Algorithm R) selects k items uniformly at random from a data stream of size n, without knowing n in advance. First, fill the reservoir with items 1..k. For item i (i>k): generate random j in [1..i]. If j≤k, replace reservoir[j-1] with item i. Each item ends up in the reservoir with probability exactly k/n.

Green = in reservoir. Yellow = being considered. Purple = replaced out. Gray = skipped.

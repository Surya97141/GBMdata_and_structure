Divide & Conquer

Closest Pair of Points

Find the two nearest points in O(n log n) using divide and conquer.

Sort points by x. Split at median. Find closest pair in left half and right half recursively. The minimum d = min(dL, dR). Then check the strip of width 2d around the dividing line — at most 8 points per strip need checking (geometric argument). Best case much faster than brute force O(n²).

Blue = left half. Red = right half. Yellow = current pair being checked. Green = current best pair. The vertical dividing line and strip are shown.

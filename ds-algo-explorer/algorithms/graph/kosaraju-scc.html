Kosaraju's SCC Algorithm

Two DFS passes — finish order on original, traverse reversed graph.

Kosaraju's finds SCCs in two DFS passes: (1) Run DFS on original graph, push nodes to a stack in finish order. (2) Transpose the graph (reverse all edges). (3) Process nodes from the stack — each DFS on the transposed graph from a new root finds one SCC.

The insight: if u can reach v in the original and v can reach u in the reversed graph, they are in the same SCC. Simpler to understand than Tarjan's but requires two full DFS passes.

O(V + E)
Space
O(V)

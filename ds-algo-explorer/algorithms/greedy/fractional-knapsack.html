Fractional Knapsack

Maximize value in capacity W — greedy: take highest value/weight ratio first.

Unlike the 0/1 knapsack (DP needed), fractional knapsack allows taking fractions of items. Sort by value/weight ratio descending. Take each item fully if it fits; otherwise take the fraction that fills remaining capacity. This greedy approach is provably optimal.

Green = taken fully. Purple = taken partially. Gray = skipped. Yellow = currently considering. The capacity bar shows remaining space.

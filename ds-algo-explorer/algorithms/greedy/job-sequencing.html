Job Sequencing with Deadlines

Maximize profit by scheduling jobs before their deadlines.

Each job has a deadline and profit. All jobs take 1 unit of time. Sort by profit descending. For each job, assign it to the latest available slot ≤ its deadline (latest-first greedy). This maximizes profit by deferring high-profit jobs as late as possible, keeping early slots for future high-profit jobs.

Green = scheduled. Yellow = currently placing. Gray = skipped (no slot available before deadline).

Time
O(n²)
With DSU
O(n log n)

Convex Hull (Graham Scan)

O(n log n) Graham Scan — sort by polar angle, maintain left-turn stack.

Graham Scan: (1) Find the bottommost point P0. (2) Sort all other points by polar angle from P0. (3) Maintain a stack — push each point. While top-3 make a right turn (clockwise), pop the middle. Only left turns (counter-clockwise) remain on the hull. O(n log n) due to sorting.

Green = on hull stack. Yellow = currently considering. Red = popped (right turn). Blue dots = sorted input points.

AVL Tree

Self-balancing BST — balance factor |BF|≤1 at every node. Guaranteed O(log n) operations.

An AVL tree is a self-balancing BST where the heights of left and right subtrees differ by at most 1 at every node. When a rotation is needed after insertion/deletion, one of four rotations (LL, RR, LR, RL) restores balance. Height is strictly O(log n), guaranteeing O(log n) worst-case search, insert, delete.

Yellow = unbalanced node. Orange = rotation pivot. Green = newly inserted. Balance factors shown above each node (height_left − height_right).
